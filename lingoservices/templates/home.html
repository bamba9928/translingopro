I am Bassirou Fall, a highly specialised professional interpreter and translator operating from Dakar, Senegal. With over nine years of frontline experience, I serve as the trusted linguistic bridge between international organisations, corporations, diplomatic missions, and local communities across West Africa.

Holding a Bachelor's degree in English Language and Linguistics from Cheikh Anta Diop University and a Postgraduate Business Diploma from the London School of Commerce, my academic foundation is matched by hands-on excellence across corporate boardrooms, hospital wards, legal proceedings, and high-profile international conferences.

I operate at the intersection of language, culture, and professional integrity — delivering consecutive, simultaneous, and liaison interpretation in English, French, and Wolof, alongside certified document translation for legal, technical, medical, and corporate fields.

"Language is the road map of a culture. I don't just translate words — I translate meaning, intent, and trust. Every assignment I undertake carries the weight of two worlds communicating."

— Bassirou Fall, Professional Interpreter & Translator
