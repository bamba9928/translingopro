Bassirou Fall: Interpreter & Translator in Senegal

Providing professional translation and interpretation services across multiple languages in Senegal and beyond.

Am Bassirou, a professional interpreter specializing in Wolof, English, and French. With over nine years of experience, I deliver high-quality interpretation and translation services tailored to the specific needs of my clients.

My expertise spans consecutive and simultaneous interpretation, technical and legal lexicon translation, business correspondence, and participation in negotiations. I have also provided interpretation services at exhibitions and in the medical field.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in English Language from Dakar University and a postgraduate diploma from London. I am fluent in Wolof, English, and French, enabling me to offer versatile and accurate language services in all three languages.

Having lived in an English-speaking country for nearly a decade and collaborated with esteemed organizations such as World Vision and Mercy Ships, I have gained invaluable experience assisting business partners during negotiations. This background allows me to consistently deliver exceptional interpretation and translation services.
